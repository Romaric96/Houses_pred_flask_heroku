HOW TO PREDICT THE PRICE OF A HOUSE

Location

The location of a property is a major factor in determining its value. Factors such as proximity to amenities, school district, crime rate, and access to public transportation can all affect the price of a home.

Property size and condition

The size and condition of a property are also important factors to consider. Larger homes generally have higher prices, but the overall condition of a property is also a critical factor. Homes that have been well-maintained and updated are likely to have higher values than those that are outdated or in disrepair.

Market trends

Real estate markets are subject to fluctuations, and it's important to consider overall market trends when making a prediction. Factors such as interest rates, supply and demand, and economic conditions can all impact the price of homes in a particular area.
